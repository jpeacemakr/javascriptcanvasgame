submarinesweeper

(An absurd game of luck and logic)

Objective:

Sink the 3 enemy submarines without hitting the 5 innocent bystanders. Sonar numbers (1-4) show how far a target is away from each shot.

Click buttons below or use the arrow keys to move the target.

Scoring:

  • +1,000: Hitting an enemy submarine
  • -100: Hitting an innocent bystander
  • -10: Missing a shot with a torpedo
